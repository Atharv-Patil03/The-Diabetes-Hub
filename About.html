About Us

The Diabetes Hub: Empowering Through Knowledge

The Diabetes Hub is an educational platform designed to provide comprehensive, accessible, and reliable information about diabetes. Created by engineering students from PVG's COET (College of Engineering and Technology), our platform aims to bridge the gap between complex medical information and everyday understanding of diabetes management.


As future engineers with a passion for healthcare technology, we recognized the need for a centralized resource that combines evidence-based medical information with practical tools for diabetes management. Our platform is designed to serve anyone affected by diabetes, from newly diagnosed patients to seasoned caregivers and healthcare professionals.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with diabetes through comprehensive education, innovative tools, and a supportive community, enabling them to make informed decisions about their health and lead fulfilling lives.


Our Vision

A world where diabetes is no longer a barrier to quality of life, where everyone affected by diabetes has access to the resources, tools, and support they need to thrive.

Our Approach

At The Diabetes Hub, we believe in:


  • Evidence-Based Information: All content on our platform is thoroughly researched and based on the latest scientific evidence and medical guidelines.
  • Accessibility: We strive to make complex medical information understandable to everyone, regardless of their medical background.
  • Practical Tools: We provide calculators, converters, and logs to help people actively manage their diabetes day-to-day.
  • Innovation: Our AI-powered chatbot offers personalized guidance and answers to specific questions about diabetes management.
  • Inclusivity: We acknowledge that diabetes affects people from all walks of life, and our content respects this diversity.
