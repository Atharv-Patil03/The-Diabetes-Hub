üçΩÔ∏è The Diabetes Plate Method

The Diabetes Plate Method is a simple way to create balanced meals without counting, measuring, or weighing food.

How to Build Your Plate

  • Fill half your plate with non-starchy vegetables (broccoli, carrots, greens, etc.)
  • Fill a quarter with lean protein (chicken, fish, tofu, eggs, etc.)
  • Fill a quarter with carbohydrates (whole grains, starchy vegetables, beans, fruit)
  • Add a small portion of healthy fats (avocado, nuts, seeds, olive oil)
  • Choose water or other zero-calorie drinks

This method automatically controls your carbohydrate intake without requiring you to count grams. It also ensures you get a good balance of nutrients at each meal.
